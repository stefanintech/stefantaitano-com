I want to build a website that outlives me.

Year ago, I set up a site using Hugo and ignored it. I want to tinker again. I want a space for myself, not for an employer. I want it to last for the people I leave behind.

I am switching from [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) to [Eleventy](https://www.11ty.dev/). I found a theme by Lene that works.

Four people inspired this project:

- [Isaac Flath](https://isaacflath.com/)
- [Dann Berg](https://dannb.org/)
- [Brian Mitchell](https://brianm.me/)
- [Lene Saile](https://www.lenesaile.com/en/)
